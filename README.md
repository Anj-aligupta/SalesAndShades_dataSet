## 🚀 Project Overview

Sales & Shades is a data analytics project focused on analyzing the performance of a D2C (Direct-to-Consumer) beauty brand. The objective of this project is to evaluate key business metrics such as revenue, conversion rate, return rate, and discount impact to derive actionable insights.

The project demonstrates how data can be used to understand customer behavior, optimize sales strategies, and improve overall business performance.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance and revenue trends
* Evaluate conversion rates and return rates
* Understand the impact of discounts on revenue
* Identify areas for improving customer retention
* Provide insights for business growth and optimization

---
